# AgriClime Sentinel - EB2-NIW Impact Statement

**AgriClime Sentinel** is an advanced atmospheric science and agricultural climate risk platform that directly serves critical U.S. national interests in:

- **Public Safety**: Severe weather prediction and early warning systems
- **National Security**: Food security and agricultural protection
- **Public Health**: Air quality monitoring and health impact assessment
- **Economic Stability**: Protection of $400+ billion agricultural sector
- **Climate Resilience**: Long-term climate change adaptation and planning
